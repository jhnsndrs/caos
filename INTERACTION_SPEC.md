# CAOS Interaction Specification

1. Home: company KPIs, accounts, alerts and capital overview.
2. Trading: performance KPIs, trade history and New Trade flow.
3. Trade Entry: form submission adds a demo trade and returns to Trading.
4. Capital: funded, challenge, cash, hedge and reserve allocation.
5. Finance: revenue, expenses, tax reserve and income sources.
6. Risk: trading permission derived from account drawdown.
7. Hedge: active hedge positions and New Hedge flow.
8. Accounts: account list; clicking an account opens Account Detail.
9. Account Detail: account health, equity and trades.
10. Challenge Detail: progress, drawdown and recommended action.
11. Payouts: New Payout updates the allocation engine.
12. Expenses: New Expense updates finance totals.
13. Reports: management KPIs and period comparisons.
14. Settings: allocation percentages and North Star are editable.

All prototype changes are stored in browser localStorage until Reset Demo is clicked.
